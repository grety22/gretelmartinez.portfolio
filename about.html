A little about me. 


I started my journey through web programming in 2006, when I made my first web project with HTML, photoshop and ASP.NET. After graduating as a technician in computer science, I studied 5 years in Telecommunications and electronics. During those years I still kept my nights doing small web pages for hobbie.

In  2015 I worked to create an eCommerce website with WordPress and Woo Commerce, integrating it with several plugins to generate catalogs and manage the products.

Then I started working for a company of shoes where I carried out several tasks of web design and front-end, such as the creation of a website for retail: www.goforshoe.com, created with Shopify, and integrated with several plugins to sell through Amazon Seller and the Amazon Affiliate Program.

In  2017 I am part of a FullStack Coding BootCamp in MDC, taught by the 4Geeks academy, where I updated my knowledge on front-end, and delve into the back-end with PHP, and the creation of templates for Wordpress from scratch.

In January of 2018 I started another BootCamp, based on MERN stack, where I finally got the underlying principles that allowed me to develop the intuition to learn new tools and technologies quickly, which I enjoy and it is one of my hobbies.
